Introduction

Welcome to the Salorid technical documentation. Our client is built on a custom kernel-level framework designed for absolute security and peak performance.

Note: Ensure your anti-virus is disabled before the initial installation to allow the driver to map correctly.

Installation

Follow these steps to get Salorid running on your system:

  1. Download the salorid_launcher.exe from your dashboard.
  2. Run as Administrator.
  3. Enter your license key when prompted.
  4. Restart your PC to finalize driver mapping.
